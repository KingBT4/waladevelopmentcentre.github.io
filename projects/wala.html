About Us

Wala development centre is an organisation started way back in 2016 on the objective of providing guidance to the community of all kinds interms of music, dance, drama and sports activities.

Welcome to our organization dedicated to fostering artistic talents and creativity in a diverse community. We believe in providing a supportive environment for individuals of all backgrounds and orientations to explore and enhance their artistic skills.

Programs

Our programs include:

  • Music: Instrumental and vocal training for all skill levels.
  • Dance: Various dance styles with professional instructors.
  • Drama: Acting workshops and theatrical productions.
  • Sports: Football, Netball, handball and basketball at all levels.

Our Inclusive Approach

We are proud to provide a welcoming and inclusive space for everyone, including the LGBTQ community. Our goal is to celebrate diversity and empower individuals through the arts.
